Pro Tricks

Queue 30 posts in Buffer

expand_more

Leverage Buffer's free plan to its maximum potential by scheduling up to 30 posts across three social channels. This is perfect for maintaining a consistent online presence without any cost.

Meta Suite for unlimited FB+IG content

expand_more

Use Meta Business Suite to schedule an unlimited number of posts, stories, and reels for your Facebook and Instagram accounts. A powerful, native tool that costs nothing.

IFTTT multi-applet hack

expand_more

Chain multiple free IFTTT applets together to create complex automation workflows. For example, automatically post your new blog article to Twitter, then have another applet share that tweet to a Discord channel.

Rotate Gmail accounts for extra workspaces

expand_more

Bypass limitations on platforms like Buffer by creating multiple free accounts using different Gmail addresses. This allows you to manage different sets of social profiles or projects separately.

Self-host n8n for infinite runs

expand_more

For the ultimate power user, self-hosting an automation tool like n8n on a cheap server gives you unlimited workflow executions and complete control over your automation stack.
